WHERE THERE'S WOOD THERE'S A WAY

With nearly a decade of experience repairing, refinishing and building guitars and wooden instruments, Ernesto is confident in his ability to bring your treasured item back to life. Trained in precise color and stain matching, crack and chip repair as well as fabrication, he approaches each job with care and attention to detail so that your antique or one of a kind item can be restored to its original splendor. Ernesto restores wood furniture, cabinetry, molding and any and al things wood.

Exact Match

1 in 12 men is affected by color blindness, Ernesto is luckily one of the 11. His highly trained eye guarantees accuracy in stain and color matching for a flawless finish.

Quality Craftsmanship

Knowing that each piece of wood and furniture is unique, Ernesto ensures a standard of workmanship through specific selection of tools to suit the style and age of the furniture.

Invisible Repairs

Using precision repair, Ernesto makes virtually invisible fixes to the damaged area, leaving the rest of the piece undisturbed and maintaining the antique's authenticy and patina.

Custom Built Designs

Do you have a picture of your dream headboard in mind but can't seem to find it anywhere? Ernesto will work with you to build to match your vision.

BRINGING HEIRLOOMS BACK TO LIFE

WITH THE DETAIL OF ORIGINAL CRAFTSMANSHIP.

ERNESTO HERNANDEZ

Ernesto has been working with wood in a professional capicity for nearly a decade, but has spent time fine-tuning his craft in his personal life for even longer. He is a certified luthier, and has spent the past several years serving musicians, repairing, refinishing and building custom instruments from multiple music stores and his own personal workshop in and around Boston, MA. Having recently moved to "Furniture City" Grand Rapids, MI, Ernesto looks forward to applying his experience, craftsmanship and attention to detail to the restoration of wood in all forms. Practiced in the precise measurements and care that instruments require to provide the highest quality sound, Ernesto has the talent and tools to bring your prized pieces back to perfection. Whether you have an antique chest worn down and chipped from generations of use or a modern desktop with the slightest surface scratch-- if it's wood, he can fix it!
