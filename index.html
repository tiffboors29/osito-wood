ABOUT


Ernesto Hernandez

Ernesto has been working with wood in a professional capicity for nearly a decade, but has spent time fine-tuning his craft in his personal life for even longer. He is a certified luthier, and has spent the past several years serving musicians, repairing, refinishing and building custom instruments from multiple music stores and his own personal workshop in and around Boston, MA. Having recently moved to "Furniture City" Grand Rapids, MI, Ernesto looks forward to applying his experience, craftsmanship and attention to detail to the restoration of wood in all forms. Practiced in the precise measurements and care that instruments require to provide the highest quality sound, Ernesto has the talent and tools to bring your prized pieces back to perfection. Whether you have an antique chest worn down and chipped from generations of use or a modern desktop with the slightest surface scratch-- if it's wood, he can fix it!
